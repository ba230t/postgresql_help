Command:     WAIT FOR
Description: wait for WAL to reach a target LSN
Syntax:
WAIT FOR LSN 'lsn'
    [ WITH ( option [, ...] ) ]

where option can be:

    MODE 'mode'
    TIMEOUT 'timeout'
    NO_THROW

and mode can be:

    standby_replay | standby_write | standby_flush | primary_flush

URL: https://www.postgresql.org/docs/19/sql-wait-for.html
